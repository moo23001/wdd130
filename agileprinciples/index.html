Agile Principles

These are the agile principles

Satisfy the customer through early and continuous delivery of valuable software

Software Delivery

Welcome changing requirements, even late in development

Welcome Change

Deliver working software frequently, from a couple of weeks to a couple of months, with a preference to the shorter timescale

Working Software

Business people and developers must work together daily throughout the project

Working Together

Build projects around motivated individuals. Give them the environment and support they need, and trust them to get the job done

Trust Teammates

The most efficient and effective method of conveying information to and within a development team is face-to-face conversation

Face 2 Face

Working software is the primary measure of progress

Progress Metrics

Agile processes promote sustainable development. The sponsors, developers, and users should be able to maintain a constant pace indefinitely

Constant Dev

Continuous attention to technical excellence and good design enhances agility

Good Design

Simplicity--the art of maximizing the amount of work not done--is essential

Simplicity

The best architectures, requirements, and designs emerge from self-organizing teams

Self-organizing

At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to become more effective, then tunes and adjusts its behavior accordingly

Retrospective

Why Agile?

In today's world adpting rapidly to the industry, market or company changes is vital. Therefore, understanding and adopting the agile culture becomes a must for companies that want to keep up with today's ever changing markets.

Agile Manifesto

The Agile Manifesto provides guidance, limits, and an understanding when everything being built or discussed. Agile presents principles not as must-have-rules, but rather as the guiding lanes on the road towards the goals we want to achive. Without principles any direction we take is likely the wrong one or the right one, depending on anyones perspective. But with principles we can know if we are building something that can be said it's going in the right direction.
