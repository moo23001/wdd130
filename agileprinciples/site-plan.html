Content

Home page

Agile can be daunting to implement, especilly if you have anti-agile practices. That's why we created this page, to help you understand its principles and know practical ways to implement them. Don't be afraid. Explore with us the road to practical agile principle implementation.

Images for the Home page

Why Agile?

This page is to explain why we have agile. Displays the main resons to implement Agile. While Agile is popular, knowing why you want to adopt agile is more important than trying to implement it. Agile is a set of principles that guide you in achiveing your ultimate goal. Think of it like using a car to get to a destination. Getting to the destination is more important than the vehicle you use. But using the correct vehicle will make it easier to get to your destination.

Images for the Why Agile page

Agile Manifesto

The manifesto is where everything branches. Each of these boxes represents guidance to the reader. Images and information are related and should be well spaced with padding like in the wireframe. Principles are essential to any organization. The Agile Manifesto provides guidance, limits, and an understanding when everything being built or discussed. Agile presents principles not as must-have-rules, but rather as the guiding lanes on the road towards the goals we want to achive. Without principles any direction we take is likely the wrong one or the right one, depending on anyones perspective. But with principles we can know if we are building something that can be said it's going in the right direction.
