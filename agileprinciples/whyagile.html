Adopting principles

While Agile is popular, knowing why you want to adopt agile is more important than trying to implement it. Agile is a set of principles that guide you in achiveing your ultimate goal. Think of it like using a car to get to a destination. Getting to the destination is more important than the vehicle you use. But using the correct vehicle will make it easier to get to your destination.

Adjusting it to your organization

Agile is adaptable. It works through agreements at all levels. Team members agree on meetings that are necessary for the team; everyone agrees when and where to review the developed software in order to understand and measure progress; clients and developers agree on deadlines and check points along the way in order to make corrections and adjust the final product.
